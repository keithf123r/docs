## What is Alice?

Alice is an AI-powered campaign management platform that helps you create, manage, and optimize your outreach campaigns. With Alice, you can:
- Create targeted campaigns using advanced audience filtering
- Customize your brand voice and communication style
- Automate campaign management with AI assistance
- Track and optimize campaign performance

## Prerequisites

Before you begin using Alice, ensure you have:

- A valid Alice account with login credentials
- Access to the Alice dashboard (https://alice.11x.ai/dashboard)
- Your brand voice and communication preferences ready
- Target audience criteria in mind

## Quick Setup Guide

### 1. Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    1. Navigate to [Alice Dashboard](https://alice.11x.ai/dashboard)
    2. Sign in with your credentials
    3. Complete your organization profile if you haven't already
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Setting Up Your Library

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Library">
    Navigate to the Library section in your dashboard to manage reusable campaign components:
    - Value propositions
    - Pain points
    - Proof points
    - Offerings
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import Components">
    1. Import existing components from your website or other sources
    2. Components can be imported by:
       - Providing a website URL for automatic extraction
       - Manual creation and customization
       - Connecting to existing components
  </Step>

  <Step title="Organize Components">
    Structure your library for efficient use:
    - Group related components
    - Add descriptions and metadata
    - Create associations between components
    - Tag components for easy search
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Brand Voice">
    Set up how Alice communicates on your behalf:
    - Define your tone (formal, casual, etc.)
    - Select writing style preferences
    - Set communication guidelines
    - Customize response patterns
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your library components and brand voice settings will be used by Alice's AI to create personalized, consistent messaging across all your campaigns.
</Note>

### 3. Creating Your First Campaign

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Define Your Audience">
    1. Use Alice's advanced filtering to define your target audience
    2. Set specific criteria like:
       - Industry categories
       - Company domains
       - Professional roles
       - Geographic locations
    3. Preview your potential audience size
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="pen-tool" title="Campaign Setup">
    - Name your campaign
    - Set campaign objectives
    - Define campaign duration
    - Configure campaign rules and constraints
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="Customize Communication">
    - Review and adjust message templates
    - Set sequence timing
    - Configure follow-up rules
    - Set up tracking parameters
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### 4. Launch and Monitor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Campaign Settings">
    Before launch, verify:
    - Audience targeting
    - Message content and sequence
    - Campaign timing and rules
    - Tracking setup
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    1. Click "Launch Campaign" in your dashboard
    2. Confirm campaign settings
    3. Start your campaign
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor Performance">
    Track key metrics in real-time:
    - Audience engagement
    - Response rates
    - Campaign progress
    - Performance metrics
  </Step>
</Steps>
